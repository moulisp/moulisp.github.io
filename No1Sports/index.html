Fitness and Sports management

No.1 Sports is a fitness and sports management firm for corporates and communities, started by a group of sports and fitness professionals.
We conduct and deliver customized fitness and sporting events according to local interest and expectations, both within and between corporates.
We bring in need based solutions with an emphasis on outcomes.

Our Objectives

Assist corporate in taking care of wellness of their employees
Minimize Occupational Hazards
Help corporates build their brand image through sports
Introduce fun at work
